Connect

Over 98% of physicians believe their patients could benefit from a clinical trial – yet fewer than 5% are ever referred into one.

How We Help
  • Increase trial awareness with HCPs
  • Find new enrollment opportunities
  • Save cost and site time

Increase Trial Awareness with HCPs

Your sites are busy. They simply do not have the time to spend reaching out to local healthcare providers to share information about your clinical trial. As a result, providers with potentially eligible patients are often unaware of studies happening on their own campus, let alone across town.

ClinOne solves this challenge by reaching out to local providers on your sites’ behalf. We automate and improve traditional “Dear Doctor” letters - featuring the goal of the study, I/E criteria, and distance to nearby enrolling sites – to drive awareness among local key opinion leaders, physicians, and specialists (e.g. Oncologists).

Find New Enrollment Opportunities

Increasing trial awareness with ClinOne can lead to new enrollment opportunities for patients who otherwise would not have known about your trial. By informing HCPs, we help expand access to entire patient populations in a much more cost-effective way than trying to reach individual patients directly.

When providers are interested in learning more, they will have a peer-to-peer conversation with your enrolling site. And because patients will find out about the trial through their trusted care team, it will lead to more informed participation decisions.

Save Cost and Set Time

ClinOne’s trial awareness solution fits inside your budget and can be up and running in as few as 2 weeks, which also makes it ideal for rescue studies. In addition to increasing awareness with local HCPs more cost-effectively than even before, it also frees time and burden from sites by taking one thing off their plate and potentially bringing enrollment opportunities directly to them.
