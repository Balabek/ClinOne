Why ClinOne

Our Mission

As CEO and Co-founder Rob Bohacswas creating ClinOne in 2016, his father was diagnosed with ALS. Rob’s in-depth knowledge of clinical research and his hands-on experience with sponsors and sites allowed him to help his parents navigate an overwhelming, stressful, and lonely process – and still they struggled. At the time, he was struck by how challenging and overwhelming it must be for people who don’t have access to a clinical research insider.

ClinOne has been on a mission ever since to drive real change by using technology to connect people, simplify site processes, and support families worldwide with a single platform and the industry’s fastest implementation.

What Sets Us Apart

Since 2016, ClinOne has supported patients and families in clinical research with a single platform for specialist referrals, eConsent, and patient empowerment.
