ClinOne Demonstrates Commitment to Customer Success with 96% On-Time Implementation of Clinical Trial Technology Platform for Study Starts in 2021

The industry’s fastest deployment averages 2-4 weeks from kickoff for remote eConsent, Uber Health, medication adherence, video visits, trial awareness campaigns, and more

January 11, 2022 – ClinOne, the patient relationship company for clinical trials, set customers up for success in 2021 by completing over 96% of study start startups of its single platform on time – often within 2-4 weeks of a customer kickoff meeting. ClinOne consistently met and exceeded deployment timelines for key elements including trial awareness, eConsent, and patient empowerment.

“Our customers inspire us by moving faster than ever to bring life-changing therapies to market, and they deserve the same commitment from their technology partners,” explained Keli Platco, (LinkedIn), ClinOne Senior VP of Operations. “That's why we feature the fastest deployment timelines in the industry - and we work closely with sponsors and CROs to ensure we meet or exceed their goals on time and on budget. It's better for everyone when we work faster together, because patients are waiting - and they can count on us.”

ClinOne customers cite its highly configurable and interoperable single platform as a key factor in meeting – and in many cases, beating – study timelines. ClinOne technology and services enable customers to address the issues that drive delays – including complex protocols, recruiting challenges, patient drop-outs, and site failures. And, because the platform does not require custom development effort for each new study, ClinOne can deliver some of the industry’s fastest deployment timelines for trial awareness, eConsent, and patient empowerment solutions including Uber Health, medication adherence, and virtual visits – even for the most complex global trials.

"The world is moving at an unprecedented pace - and while matching that speed is important, it is essential to deliver on our quality promises," Platco added. "I'm proud that we have earned the trust of our customers and we are honored to work alongside so many leading sponsors, CROs, and sites as they accelerate efforts to bring new treatments to patients who need them."

About ClinOne

ClinOne is the patient relationship company for clinical trials. More than 50 leading pharmaceutical companies and CROs trust us to CONNECT, INFORM, and EMPOWER patients to help them take control of their clinical trial experience. Our single technology platform includes proven solutions for peer referrals, eConsent, virtual visits, Uber Health, dosing management, eDiaries, and more – featuring unrivaled remote capabilities, cost-efficient deployment, and the industry’s fastest implementation timelines. ClinOne is led by a team of global experts with decades of success running global trials for many of the largest technology providers.
